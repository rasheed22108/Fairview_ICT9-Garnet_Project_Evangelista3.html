Ways to Prevent Fire in your own Home


A fire needs three elements - heat, oxygen and fuel. Without heat, oxygen and fuel a fire will not start or spread. A key strategy to prevent fire is to remove one or more of heat, oxygen or fuel. The risk assessment should include detail on all three elements to minimise the risk of a fire starting/spreading.

Store flammable products correctly Hairspray and shaving cream can be dangerous when exposed to fire. You should store household cleaning items or items such as hairspray and shaving cream correctly.
Maintain your wires Damaged wires can be a fire hazard and need to be replaced as soons as possible. You should maintain your wires frequently.
Install smoke alarms Installing smoke alarms could help you be alerted when there is a fire. You should check the fire alarms every month to see if it is working.
Be Cautious with open flames Open flames could be very hazardous when it is left alone. Put out open flames immediately after use to prevent a fire.
Keep a fire extinguisher and a blanket at all times A fire extinguisher and a blanket could help you prevent your house burning. You should keep these things to extinguish the fire.
